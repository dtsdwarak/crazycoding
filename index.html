Janani Lakshmanamurthy

Web Developer

Hi, I am passionate about building websites and love playing around with the jQuery and CSS. I have also been trained in Java and portlets and have some hands-on on a WCM Tool called CQ. My basics of programming have rooted right from C,C++ and Java and now am in the process of learning Ruby too. I am currently working in Cognizant and pursuing my Masters in a Work Integrated Learning Program from BITS, Pilani. Using a Content Management tool to develop websites has helped me spread my domain of learning both in the front end and back end web development. Quite recently, I have been working on Responsive Web Design too.

What do I work In?

Digital Enhancement & Content Management

I work for a Pharmaceutical client, AstraZeneca, where we build websites and develop campaigns for their multiple brands with special functionalities to attract the consumers and also the physicians.
